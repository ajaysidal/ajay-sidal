# Phase 2 Execution Roadmap — 7 Days

## Day 1
- Finalize homepage messaging and investor brief
- Confirm backup and rollback readiness

## Day 2
- Polish demo flow and booking journey
- Prepare founder narrative and call opening

## Day 3
- Build target investor list by fit and warm-intro path
- Prepare personalized outreach notes

## Day 4
- Start founder-led outreach to the top tier of aligned angels and operators
- Capture responses and objections in one place

## Day 5
- Run live demos and collect feedback
- Tighten positioning based on call notes

## Day 6
- Package traction proof, product screenshots, and operational readiness items
- Prepare use-of-funds framing

## Day 7
- Hold second-wave investor conversations
- Review what landed, what stalled, and what needs sharper proof

## Core rule
Lead with execution, clarity, and repeatable revenue. Keep MARZ positioned as a thoughtful moat, not the headline risk.
