# Data Room Checklist — April 2026

## Must-have assets before serious investor calls

### Product
- homepage screenshots
- investor brief screenshots
- dashboard and identity flow screenshots
- clean demo sequence notes

### Commercial
- pricing summary
- revenue model one-pager
- target customer description
- pilot and lead pipeline snapshot

### Technical
- architecture summary
- security posture summary
- uptime / health readiness evidence
- deployment and rollback notes

### Founder materials
- founder bio
- mission and market thesis
- why now narrative
- use-of-funds outline

### Optional but high value
- short Loom demo
- customer or advisor quote
- one-page roadmap
- market comparison slide

## Rule
Do not send a full data room too early. Start with the investor brief and only expand once interest is confirmed.
