# Founder Call Script and Objection Handling

## 30-second opening
We are building Build With AI, an AI-native registrar and infrastructure platform for modern businesses. Instead of forcing teams to stitch together domains, security, hosting, and identity across multiple vendors, we bring that into one product system. MARZ supports the ecosystem as a utility and retention layer rather than a hype-first token story.

## 3-minute narrative
1. State the problem clearly
2. Show the product and why the experience is better
3. Explain the revenue model in plain language
4. Position MARZ as a moat, not the core risk
5. Close with the ask and the type of support you want

## Demo sequence
- Homepage and value proposition
- Product catalog and service breadth
- Identity and wallet-native layer
- Dashboard and operating depth
- Investor brief and roadmap

## Common objections and responses

### This feels crowded
Response:
Most players are either old registrars with weak UX or Web3 products without strong utility. Our wedge is the combination of practical infrastructure revenue plus a modern identity layer.

### Why does Web3 belong here?
Response:
We are not forcing a token on users. We are using wallet-native identity and ecosystem utility where it adds retention, access, and long-term platform value.

### Is this token-dependent?
Response:
No. The SaaS business stands on its own through domains, SSL, hosting, and services. MARZ strengthens the ecosystem but does not replace the core business model.

### How do you go to market?
Response:
Founder-led sales, productized service offers, pilot customers, and partner-driven distribution. We focus on a narrow early customer before broadening.

### Why now?
Response:
AI has changed user expectations, but registrar and infrastructure experiences have barely evolved. That gap creates a strong timing advantage.

## Call close options
Ask for one next step only:
- a second diligence call
- an intro to a relevant investor or partner
- product feedback from an operator

## Tone rules
- calm
- concrete
- revenue-aware
- never defensive
- never over-sell the token layer
