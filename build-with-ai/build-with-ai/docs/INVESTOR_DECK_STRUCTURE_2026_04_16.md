# Investor Deck Structure — Build With AI

## Positioning
Build With AI is the AI-native registrar and infrastructure platform for modern businesses. We combine domains, security, hosting, and wallet-ready identity in one product system.

## Recommended 12-slide structure

1. Cover
   - Company name
   - One-line thesis
   - Founder name and contact

2. The Problem
   - Digital infrastructure is fragmented
   - Legacy registrars still rely on upsells and slow workflows

3. The Product
   - Domains
   - SSL and protection
   - Hosting and provisioning
   - Wallet-native identity

4. Why This Wins
   - Faster setup
   - Cleaner trust layer
   - Better retention through ecosystem benefits

5. Ideal Customer
   - Founders
   - Agencies
   - Service-led SMBs

6. Business Model
   - Recurring infrastructure revenue
   - Productized service revenue
   - Expansion through premium features and retention layers

7. MARZ Utility
   - Access
   - Rewards
   - Loyalty
   - Governance-lite participation

8. Market Timing
   - AI adoption
   - Need for operational simplicity
   - Renewed interest in practical Web3 utility

9. Traction and Readiness
   - Product live
   - Payments active
   - Identity integrated
   - Monitoring and deployment safeguards in place

10. Go-to-Market
   - Founder-led sales
   - Productized demo flow
   - Partnerships and ecosystem visibility

11. Roadmap
   - Pilot conversions
   - Analytics and funnel expansion
   - Partnerships and institutional credibility

12. Ask
   - Capital ask
   - Use of funds
   - Strategic help requested

## Tone guidance
- Lead with commercial clarity
- Keep token language conservative
- Show product and execution before vision language
