Ajay is building Build With AI, an AI-native registrar and infrastructure platform that combines domains, security, hosting, and wallet-ready identity in one system. The product is already live and the team is opening pilot conversations with aligned angels and strategic partners.

Subject: Founder-led infrastructure platform at the AI and Web3 intersection

Best,
Ajay

## Follow-up sequence
- Day 1: Initial outreach
- Day 4: Short product update with one proof point
- Day 8: Final follow-up with direct booking link

## Founder demo structure for calls
1. Open with the thesis in 30 seconds
2. Show homepage and product clarity
3. Show identity and wallet-native layer
4. Show dashboard and operational depth
5. Explain revenue model simply
6. Position MARZ as utility and retention, not hype
7. Close with specific ask

## Demo close
Ask for one of three outcomes:
- follow-up diligence call
- intro to an aligned investor
- strategic product feedback
