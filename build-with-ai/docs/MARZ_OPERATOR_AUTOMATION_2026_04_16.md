# MARZ Operator Automation — Founder Approval Mode

## What is now active
- new investor and sales opportunities are captured automatically
- follow-up tasks are queued in the Command Center
- founder approval can now trigger the next outreach action
- booking and calendar invite creation are available through the app workflow

## Current operating mode
Human-approved automation.

That means MARZ can prepare the next action, but Ajay remains in command for approval until the automation policy is mature enough for broader autonomy.

## Approval flow
1. opportunity enters the queue
2. MARZ prepares the suggested action
3. founder approves the task from the Command Center
4. outreach is sent through the active mail transport or safely drafted
5. calendar and follow-up records are updated

## Why this is the right mode now
- lowers risk
- keeps founder voice intact
- still saves significant time
- creates a clear audit trail for every outbound action
