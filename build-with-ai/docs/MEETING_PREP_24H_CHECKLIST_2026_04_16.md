# 24-Hour Meeting Prep Checklist

## The day before the call
- review the investor's latest posts, portfolio, and thesis
- tailor the opening sentence to them
- confirm the demo path works in preview
- prepare one clear ask for the end of the call

## 1 hour before the call
- open the homepage
- open the investor brief
- open the identity flow
- open the leads booking page
- keep pricing and roadmap docs ready

## During the call
- open with the problem and product
- show the product quickly
- explain the business model clearly
- keep MARZ positioned as utility, not speculation
- ask for one next step only

## After the call
- log notes immediately
- send a concise thank-you
- share one tailored follow-up asset
- schedule the next touchpoint within 7 days
